This is a list of references in Cool and New Webcomic music, working on the same principles as this webpage listing references in Homestuck music.

It doubles as a list of songs by composer and track artist. Thanks to wheals for hosting this page, helping with HTML coding, and making a few other edits. Additional edits by ViKomprenas and power464646.

Songs that contain references have their titles in bold.
Referenced songs on Cool and New Webcomic albums are in orange.
Referenced songs on official Homestuck albums are in blue.
Referenced songs on unofficial Homestuck albums (i.e., those on Lambda's list) and not on CANWC albums are in red.
All other referenced Homestuck-related songs (including those on unofficial music team albums not available on Bandcamp, or music for other fanventures) are in pink.
All other referenced songs are in green.
Stuff I'm not sure about is italicized. Feel free to correct those, or any other mistakes I may have made for that matter, or clarify how exactly it's referenced.

An italicized song followed by (??) means I have no idea where the song is referenced.

Some songs with long names are abbreviated the same way as on the webpage listed above, with one addition: RollerCoaster Tycoon is abbreviated as RCT.
